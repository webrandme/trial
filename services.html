Common Questions

FAQs

Have questions before reaching out? Here are the answers to our most frequently asked questions.

What is your pricing structure? +

Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees. We offer three website packages: Standard (basic single-page websites), Premium (single-page with luxury animations), and Elite (multi-page with high-end animations).Our Pricing starts from ₹5999. Each package includes ongoing support. For custom projects, we provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements during our discovery call. Your budget is always our priority.

How long does a typical project take? +

Project timeline depends on scope and complexity. Standard websites typically take 7-10 days, Premium websites 10-12 days, and Elite multi-page websites apprx. 15 days. We always provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase and respect all deadlines. Regular progress updates keep you informed throughout the project lifecycle.

How do I get started? +

Getting started is simple! Fill out the contact form above or book a free Quick Call with our team. During this 30-minute call, we'll discuss your project, understand your vision, understand your goals, and provide a clear roadmap and transparent pricing. No obligation, just a friendly conversation about how we can help your business grow online.

Do you provide post-launch support? +

Absolutely! We provide dedicated support after launch. Our team is available for maintenance, updates, bug fixes, and any technical assistance you might need. We consider ourselves your long-term digital partner, not just a one-time vendor. You'll have direct access to our support team whenever you need help.

Is the initial consultation free? +

Yes! Our initial Quick Call consultation is completely free, with no hidden charges or obligations. We'll learn about your project, discuss your vision and goals, answer your questions, and provide a preliminary overview of how we can help. There's absolutely no pressure to sign up. This is our way of understanding if we're the right fit for your project.
